About Me

Learn more about my journey and expertise

I'm Meet, a Data Analyst and Junior Data Engineer based in Houston. I work at the intersection of data engineering and analytics - building the pipelines that move and clean data, and the dashboards and models that make sense of it.

When I joined Archi Farming Equipment, their demand planning was largely guesswork. I worked with the team to build time-series forecasting models in Python that improved prediction accuracy by 22% and brought stock-outs down by 18%. I also helped automate their reporting workflows - what used to take hours every week got reduced by 40%, and 10+ stakeholders finally had a single source of truth they could trust on Monday morning.

Beyond analysis, I've been deep in the engineering side too. I've built cloud pipelines on Azure - Data Factory pulling raw data into ADLS Gen2, transforming it in Databricks with PySpark, and surfacing it through Synapse Analytics. I've done the same on AWS using Lambda, Glue, and Athena. These aren't toy projects - they're the kind of infrastructure that makes sure clean, structured data is always where it needs to be.

More recently I've been exploring agentic AI - built a stock recommendation system using LangChain and LangGraph that autonomously researches market data and surfaces investment insights. It's the direction I see data and AI heading, and I want to be ahead of it.

I build solutions that solve real problems and create measurable impact through data and AI. Let's connect!
